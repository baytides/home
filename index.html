Our Mission

At Bay Tides, we're passionate about protecting the natural beauty and health of the San Francisco Bay. Through hands-on projects, educational programs, and collaborative conservation efforts, we empower residents to make a meaningful impact in their backyard.

Learn More About Us

What We Do

Our approach to protecting the San Francisco Bay

📚

Educate

Through workshops, community partnerships, and public programs, we provide accessible education on environmental issues and conservation practices.

📣

Advocate

We empower residents to champion policies and initiatives that protect the bay's future, engaging with local leaders and environmental organizations.

🌱

Action

From habitat restoration to volunteer-led conservation efforts, we mobilize our community to make a tangible difference for the Bay Area.

Why Our Work Matters

The San Francisco Bay is more than just a natural landmark—it's a vital ecosystem that protects critical and endangered wildlife and enriches our community.

With your support, we can ensure a thriving, resilient Bay Area for generations to come.
