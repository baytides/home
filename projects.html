Bay Area Discounts

Helping residents discover savings programs across the Bay Area.

We started Bay Area Discounts with a simple observation: financial barriers often prevent people from making sustainable choices . When money is tight, public transit, energy-efficient upgrades, and outdoor recreation can feel out of reach.

As we built the tool, we realized its potential extended far beyond environmental programs. The Bay Area is one of the most expensive regions in the country, and residents deserve a single place to find all the assistance available to them.

So we expanded. Today, Bay Area Discounts covers:

  • Transit savings — discounted fares for Clipper, BART, Caltrain, and more
  • Utility assistance — programs like CARE and FERA for energy bills
  • Community resources — libraries, food assistance, and local services
  • Parks and recreation — free and discounted access to public lands
  • Healthcare and wellness — sliding-scale and low-cost programs

A stronger community starts with residents who can thrive. By connecting people with resources, we're building a more resilient Bay Area—and staying true to our environmental mission along the way.

How Discounts Help the Environment

The connection between affordability and sustainability

🚌

Reduced Emissions

Transit discounts make public transportation affordable, reducing car trips and lowering carbon emissions across the Bay Area.

⚡

Energy Conservation

Utility assistance programs like CARE and FERA help residents afford energy-efficient upgrades and reduce overall energy consumption.

🌳

Nature Connection

Free park passes and outdoor recreation discounts help residents connect with nature, fostering the environmental stewardship we need.
